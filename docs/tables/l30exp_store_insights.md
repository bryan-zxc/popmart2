## Key Notes

- Top 10 and bottom 10 physical stores by revenue (20 total)
- Online store excluded from ranking to compare physical stores only
- Includes dominant_category showing highest revenue category for each store
- Performance_group field distinguishes Top 10 vs Bottom 10
- Enables understanding of what drives success at top performers
- Helps assess which bottom performers might benefit from online conversion

## Purpose

Focused analysis for Store Performance initiative - highlights best practices from top performers and identifies intervention opportunities for bottom performers.
